Khabardar is a browser extension that warns you against unreliable news sources.

Download

How It Works

Khabardar works using a series of filters. Each filter searches the website for cues that would indicate an unreliable source of information.

Age Filter

Websites that are less than a year old are generally unreliable and shouldn't be trusted.

Blog Filter

Blogs hosted on blogging networks like blogspot and tumblr pretending to be news sites.

CrowdSourced Blacklist

A blacklist of unreliable websites that are known to spread false and fake news.
